Home Sweet Home

Web application designed for Bitcamp 2022 - encompassed compiling real estate data into a database (ETL, loaded to CockroachDB), assembling queries to source information, and designing the frontend. Users would be directed to a quiz from the homepage, each quiz question was linked to an attribute of the locations stored in the dataset. Furthermore users could organize the filters dependent upon what they prioritized in a home. After submission, users would be redirected to their results (top 10 locations that matched their filters) populated on a Google Map.

**Tech Stack: SQL, Python (Pandas, NumPy, Django), HTML, CSS, GoogleMaps API, CockroachDB**

UCEducations Tutoring System

Web portal application for IS Undergraduate Capstone (2020), designed for UCEducations - designed and implemented a database system and web portal for a client in the education industry. Functionalities included user-specific views(management, student, tutor), ability for management to match students and tutors, ability for students to take automatically graded assessments, ability for students and tutors to schedule appointments, and ability for management to view student progress to evaluate tutor effectiveness.

**Tech Stack: Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, PHP, SQL, SQLServer**
